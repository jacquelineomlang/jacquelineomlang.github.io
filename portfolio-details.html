PROJECT INFORMATION


  • Project title: StarCine
  • Project date: June 2023

"StarCine" is an advanced cinema ticket booking reservation system located in SM Mall Butuan. It operates as an online platform that offers users a convenient and efficient way to explore a wide range of movies, make their preferred selections, and securely complete the reservation process. StarCine endeavors to provide customers with a smooth and stress-free ticket reservation experience.
